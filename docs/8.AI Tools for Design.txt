PIXELCUT:
           Pixelcut, now rebranded as Pixa, is a widely used AI-powered creative app available on Android and iOS that helps millions of users with tasks like background removal, object erasing, AI product photoshoots, image upscaling, and even AI-generated copywriting; while Pixelcut originally focused on photo editing, Pixa expands into video workflows and creative automation, making it especially popular among e-commerce sellers and social media creators who need quick, professional visuals and ads.
