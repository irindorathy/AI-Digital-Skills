Microsoft Power BI is a business analytics tool developed by Microsoft that helps users analyze and visualize data. It allows people to connect data from different sources such as Excel files, databases, and online services. Using Power BI, users can clean, organize, and transform data and then create interactive charts, graphs, and dashboards. These visual reports make it easier to understand information and find useful insights. Power BI is widely used by students, businesses, and organizations to make better decisions based on data.(UMMUL NAJIHA) 📊
 Microsoft Power BI** is widely used for analyzing and visualizing data in many fields such as business, finance, healthcare, education, and marketing. It helps organizations convert raw data into meaningful insights through interactive dashboards, charts, and reports. Power BI can connect to different data sources like Excel files, databases, and cloud services to analyze large amounts of information quickly. Companies use it to monitor sales performance, track financial data, study customer behavior, and make better decisions based on real-time data analysis.(valarmathi)
Microsoft Power BI plays a vital role both in everyday work and in business by transforming raw data into clear, interactive dashboards that help people make faster decisions. In daily life, individuals and teams use it to track progress, budgets, or performance without relying on static spreadsheets, while in business it integrates data from multiple sources—like sales, finance, marketing, and operations—into one platform, enabling companies to monitor KPIs, forecast trends, detect inefficiencies, and share insights across departments. This combination of accessibility, automation, and real-time analysis makes Power BI a powerful tool for improving productivity and driving smarter strategies at every level.(Velmurugan)
