Artificial Intelligence (AI) in Microsoft Word, Excel, and PowerPoint has transformed the way we create documents, analyze data, and design presentations. In Word, AI assists with grammar and spelling corrections, suggests better sentence structures, summarizes long texts, and can even generate content like reports or letters. In Excel, AI helps analyze large datasets, detect patterns, suggest formulas, automate data entry with Flash Fill, and forecast trends, making data handling faster and more accurate. In PowerPoint, AI aids in designing professional slides, recommending layouts, generating slides from text, suggesting relevant images, and providing speaker coaching to improve presentations. Overall, AI makes Microsoft Office tools smarter, increases productivity, reduces errors, and allows users to focus more on creativity and decision-making rather than repetitive tasks.(Srividhya vahini A R)
