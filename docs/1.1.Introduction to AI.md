AI is used in many areas of everyday life. For example, virtual assistants, recommendation systems on streaming platforms, self-driving cars, medical diagnosis systems, and fraud detection in banking all rely on AI technologies. These systems help automate complex tasks, improve efficiency, and support human decision-making.Despite its many benefits, AI also raises important questions about privacy, job displacement, ethics, and security. As AI continues to develop, researchers and policymakers are working to ensure that it is used responsibly and for the benefit of society.
