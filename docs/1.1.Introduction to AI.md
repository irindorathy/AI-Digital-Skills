Artificial Intelligence (AI) is a field of computer science that focuses on creating machines and software capable of performing tasks that normally require human intelligence. These tasks include learning from data, recognizing patterns, understanding language, solving problems, and making decisions. AI systems use technologies such as machine learning, deep learning, natural language processing, and computer vision to analyze large amounts of data and improve their performance over time. Today, AI is widely used in many areas such as healthcare, finance, education, transportation, and online services, helping automate processes, increase efficiency, and support better decision-making.
