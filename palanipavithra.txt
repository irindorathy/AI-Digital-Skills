AI Fluency Skills empower individuals to understand, communicate, and work effectively with artificial intelligence technologies. It is not just about coding or technical expertise, but about developing the ability to interpret AI outputs, recognize its limitations, and apply AI tools responsibly in everyday tasks. By building fluency, learners gain confidence in collaborating with intelligent systems, making informed decisions, and adapting to the rapidly evolving digital landscape where AI is becoming a core skill for education, careers, and innovation.
